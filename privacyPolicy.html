PRIVACY POLICY

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully as it describes the way in which we collect and handle your personal information for the following services: Cinema, Rewards, Careers and any other services.We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time by updating this page. The revised Privacy Policy will take effect when it is posted on our website.

We refer throughout this Privacy Policy to ‘personal information’ which means information which identifies you as an individual or from which you can reasonably be identified. Your name, email, address, telephone numbers, billing or purchase information are all examples of ‘personal information’.

How and what personal information do we collect

We collect your personal information in a number of ways directly from you, including through website purchases, employment applications and phone calls and emails. The types of personal information we collect includes your email address, name, address, date of birth, phone number, mobile number and other contact details.

Purpose for collecting your personal information

We collect your personal information to provide you with our Services and to help us provide you with product and service information tailored to your interests and to provide you with a customised experience across the whole range of our services.

Access and Correction of the personal information about you

You can acces your personal information by visiting our website and also correct your personal information that is inaccurate or out-of-date. Alternatively, you can access or correct your personal information by contacting us directly over a phone or by email.

Storage and security of your personal information

We take all reasonable steps to protect all of the personal information we hold from misuse and loss, and from unauthorised access. For example, access to your personal information is restricted to employees who have specific authorisation to access such information.

Links to other websites

We provides links to websites that are owned and/or operated by third parties. Unless otherwise stated at those websites, those websites are not under our control, and we are not responsible for the conduct of companies linked to our website. Before disclosing your personal information on any other website, we advise you to examine the terms and conditions of those websites.

What to do if you wish to make a complaint or have a query

If you wish to make a complaint about the way in which we handle your personal information, or would like to access or correct your personal information, please contact us at info@cinema.com.
